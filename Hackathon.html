VIT Hackathon

The ultimate goal of the hackathon is to create a platform to built innovative ideas as a well suitable systems to address a broad spectrum social issues. The hackathon is a small step towards the zealous courses taken by science and technology intended to escalate the adoption of more and more application based projects which can help the students as engineers to become well suited for industry.

Hackathon Event Guidelines & Timeline

  • It will be a 24-hours Hackathon consisting of 3 rounds.
  • Expected turnout is more than 1000 students in terms of 3-5 members each.
  • Problem Statement will be assigned according to the theme.
  • Teams will work according to the given statements and at the end of the first round,
    they will pitch their ideas with the abstracts to the panel.

Prizes:

Below are the prize for top winners under different categories
  • 1st Prize-Rs 50,000
  • 2nd Prize-Rs 30,000
  • 3rd Prize-Rs 20,000
