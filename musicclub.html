Music Club

Music club is dedicated to improve, refine and develop the music skill among the students of VIT Chennai. It provides opportunity to the music aspirants by conducting and organizing various events. It encourages the students to participate and compete at various platforms. The primary objective of club is to explore new talents and impart the qualities to the students. It also includes conduction and organization of such events that create an atmosphere of fun and excitement to the student members. Events such as Pitch please, Retro night, Poco andante, Battle of bands, Supersinger and a lot of exciting and enthralling activities which not only boost the confidence but also help in ensuring the mastery in forte.
